Oreo Cheesecake

Description

This Oreo cheesecake is easy to prepare and a great dessert to bring to your next get-together.

This Oreo cheesecake is smooth, rich, and full of indulgent cookies and creme flavor.

Ingredients

  • Oreos:You'll need about 24 Oreos (16 for the crust and eight for the filling).
  • Butter: Melted butter gives the crust moisture and helps bind the cookie crumbs together.
  • Cream Cheese: The Oreo cheesecake filling starts with three packages of cream cheese.
  • Sugar: The Oreo cheesecake filling starts with three packages of cream cheese.
  • Vanilla: Vanilla extract enhances the flavor of the decadent cheesecake.
  • Eggs: Whole eggs lend moisture and help bind the cheesecake batter together.

Steps

  1. Crush 16 cookies and make the crust.
  2. Make the filling, then mix in half of the remaining chopped cookies.
  3. Pour the filling into the crust and sprinkle with the remaining chopped cookies.
  4. Bake until the center is set. Refrigerate until the cheesecake is completely set.
